BETH DOLCE

ABOUT ME

My appreciation of food as medicine has evolved from many years of studying nutrition and working with over 200 individuals as a wellness and fitness coach to help them better understand their bodies nutritional and wellness needs.

Early on in my 20's I was challenged to get to the root cause of my own body's ailments. I dealt with gut discomfort fo ryears and after numerous doctors handing me prescriptions that did nothing to cure my discomfort, I decided to take initiative and ownership of my body's cry for help. After many food elimination trials and errors, I was able to determine my food sensitivites and finally regain control over how food made me feel. Becoming my own advocate for my physical resiliency is what became my motivation to learn as much as I could to guide others to find their resilient wellness.

I believe in the importance of nourishing our bodies with whole foods that are not only healthy, but taste delicious. Through my personal love of food and cooking, I educate and teach individuals how to understand foods, plan, cook and enjoy healthy meals at home and on the go. I believe the more you fall in love with food and what it can do for your well being then the more you will fall in love with YOU!
